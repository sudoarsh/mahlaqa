Open Data, Methodology, and Computational Scripts

The Methodology

Base text selection: SJ 1818 is the base text. Reasons: last manuscript under authorial authority, consistent with earlier witnesses, most complete at 125 ghazals.

Emendation policy: Conservative. SJ emended only where demonstrably broken by scribal error and where older manuscripts supply a coherent reading.

Levenshtein calculation: Normalized character-level Levenshtein distance. Normalization: distance divided by length of longer string.
