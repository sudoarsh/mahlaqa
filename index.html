دیوانِ چندا — تنقیدی ایڈیشن

ماہ لقا بائی چندا کے چاروں مخطوطاتی مآخذ کا پہلا منظم تقابل

This website is the digital companion to the critical edition of Diwan-e-Chanda by Mah Laqa Bai Chanda (c. 1768–1820/21). It presents, for the first time, a complete systematic collation of all known witnesses to her diwan: the British Library manuscript of 1799, the Telangana Oriental manuscript of 1811, the Salar Jung manuscript of 1818, and the 1906 lithograph print edition Gulzar-e-Mah Laqa.

The collation reveals that the 1906 print edition — which has served as the basis of every subsequent edition for 120 years — is a posthumous editorial corruption that systematically replaced Mah Laqa Bai's Dakhni idiom with northern Urdu. This edition restores her authentic text from manuscript evidence for the first time since her death.

All data underlying this edition is publicly available on this site and on GitHub. Scholars are invited to verify, challenge, and build upon these findings.
