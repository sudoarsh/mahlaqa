About the Project

This project presents a rigorous critical edition of the Diwan of Mah Laqa Bai Chanda, the pioneering 18th-century Urdu poetess. Utilizing the Lachmann method of textual criticism and structural paleography, this digital edition systematically collates multiple historical witnesses to trace the textual transmission and stylistic evolution of her ghazals.

The Witnesses

  • BL (1799): Early manuscript housed at the British Library.
  • TO (1811): Manuscript preserved in the Telangana state archives.
  • SJ (1818): Later manuscript from the Salar Jung collection.
  • GM: A subsequent print edition serving as the base text for variant comparison and diff highlighting.
