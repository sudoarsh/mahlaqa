Diwan of Mah Laqa Bai Chanda

A Digital Critical & Genetic Edition

Edited by Akash Tiwari

Read the Collation Codicological Survey

A Structural Intervention in Tadveen-e-Matn

This project is the first systematic, forensically verified critical edition of the complete poetic works of Mah Laqa Bai Chanda (1768–1824). Moving beyond passive reliance on late print traditions, this digital archive bypasses a century of colonial-era institutional censorship to restore her original 18th-century authorial voice.

By providing a line-by-line, auditable Apparatus Criticus, this open-data companion to the print edition shifts textual criticism from an unverifiable monolith into a transparent, living repository.
